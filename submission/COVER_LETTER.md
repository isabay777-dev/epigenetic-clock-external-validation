Dear Biogerontology Editor,

Please consider the manuscript titled "External validation, conformal uncertainty, and feature stability in machine-learning epigenetic clocks trained on established methylation panels" for publication as a Research Article in Biogerontology.

This study provides a reality-check analysis of epigenetic-clock modeling on established methylation clock-CpG panels. Using two public whole-blood GEO cohorts and bidirectional external validation, the manuscript shows that regularized linear models match or outperform more complex machine-learning models in this setting. It also reports calibrated-uncertainty analyses using split-conformal prediction and feature-stability analyses using SHAP, with cautious interpretation of transfer asymmetry and CpG-attribution stability.

The manuscript is original, is not under consideration elsewhere, and has not been published previously. The submission is single-authored. The study uses only public, de-identified Gene Expression Omnibus data from GSE40279 and GSE87571, with no new human subjects, biospecimens, or identifiable participant data.

Thank you for your consideration.
